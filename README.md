# AI_MathMentor

An interactive AI-powered chatbot to help students practice mathematics from **1st to 10th standard**, with step-by-step explanations via a clean and simple interface.

---

## Problem Statement

Many students between 1st and 10th standard face difficulties in learning and practicing mathematics. Traditional learning methods often lack **step-by-step explanations**, immediate feedback, and an interactive way to solve doubts. As a result, students struggle with concepts and lose interest in mathematics.

---

## Solution

**AI_MathMentor** is designed to solve this issue by:  
- Providing an **interactive chatbot** for practicing math problems.  
- Offering **step-by-step solutions** instead of just final answers.  
- Covering math topics from **basic arithmetic to advanced concepts (1st to 10th standard)**.  
- Allowing students to learn **anytime, anywhere** with a responsive and simple UI.
